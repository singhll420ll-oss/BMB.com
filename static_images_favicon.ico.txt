Instructions for favicon.ico:
1. Create a simple icon based on your logo
2. Multiple sizes needed: 16x16, 32x32, 64x64 pixels
3. ICO format (or PNG for modern browsers)
4. Save as: static/images/favicon.ico

For quick generation:
- Use your logo simplified
- Single color (orange #ff6b35) on transparent
- Burger or bite mark symbol
